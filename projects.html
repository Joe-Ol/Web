Swahili Word Embeddings Using Word2Vec

A demo on training word embeddings which can be used in place of Tf-Idf / BoW feature extraction approaches. This technique presents a words as a multi-dimension vector which allows us to capture word-to-word co-relations hence capturing context within text. If two words have similar meaning they will lie close to each other in the multi-dimension vector space
